About

I am a fresh graduate with a bachelors of science in mechanical engineering, with a concentration in material science. I have a passion for learning, which is why I taught myself python and CNC toolpathing. The majority of my work experience is in my university 3D printing lab, where I managed and maintained over 30 printers of various ecosystems, including Stratasys and Prusa Research. That position gave me in depth knowledge of part design and additive manufacturing, as well as first hand experience with subtractive manufacturing due to working closely with the university fabrication shop. As a side project on my personal time, I also conducted material strength studies on 3D printed test samples, to determine the strength differences between materials, colors, and brands. While on my personal time, I did this project to better my work to give myself a better understanding of which material was best for each application.
